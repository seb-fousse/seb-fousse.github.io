Hiking With Dad

HackBeanpot 2021 Runner-Up

Hiking with dad was a project made in 36 hours for HackBeanpot. The hackathon theme was camping, with an emphasis sustainability, community, and outdoorsy-ness. The chatbot takes in a location, future date, and trail type, and will give you a packing list for a day hike based on weather conditions. The chatbot is meant to be your “dad“ and you are their child who wants to go on a hike with them. Dad will tell random jokes, and has an extensive series of conversation options. This was my first time using react and my first time doing any web dev. At the time, I was mostly used to OOP type stuff. I ended up using a chatbot API as well as a weather API. I was quite proud of being able to make this in such little time, and really happy to have won the runner up prize!
