Tables

Showcase

I needed a set of shelves for my closet, as I had no dresser or place to put my tees and pants. I wanted to build something that could function as a low shelf or as a side/end table, while not using any screws or nails. This project was a practice in handcutting dado joints, and I feel as though I improved slightly. Two tables were built, but only one is pictured.
