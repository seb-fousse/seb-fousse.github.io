Musing Man Winkle

An Audio-Visual Self Portrait

An audio-visual portrait I created using machine learning tools and editing software. The audio is made up of clips from assorted childhood home videos, representing a blend of experiences and sounds I absorbed when I was younger. Synced to the audio is a constantly morphing face that changes in age, expression, and quality. This face is generated from photos of me as an infant, toddler, child, teenager, and adult. I am a collection of my experiences, and they are the building blocks for the current iteration of me
